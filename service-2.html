01

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

We delight ourselves in the specialty of project management that ensures that the project is delivered on time, within cost, and to the required quality. This is achieved through:

  • Coordinating the design team through the pre-tender stage to achieve approved drawings for the fit-out, to achieve all the requirements in the Client’s brief;
  • Coordinating the design team through the tendering stage to ensure that the right construction companies and specialty sub-contractors are vetted, selected, negotiated, and approved to proceed with the proposed construction;
  • Coordinating the design team through the post-contract stage to ensure that progress on site is as per the approved schedule (program of works), managing various project risks that might delay the project or cause the project to have cost overruns, whilst monitoring the quality of workmanship and materials being utilized on the project to adhere to the required specifications and highest standards;
  • Coordinating project closure stage to ensure that all project deliverables and construction have been carried out, testing and commissioning done to confirm the functionality of the built product, following up with relevant stakeholders to ensure that all necessary completion certificates, warranties, and compliance certificates have been availed to the Client;
  • Coordinating user training to ensure that the client and the staff are well-trained on how to use the installed equipment.

QUANTITY SURVEYING

Our services for Quantity surveying include:

PRE- CONTRACT

  • Advice on the most economic designs
  • Preparation of cost estimates
  • Cost engineering to meet client’s budget
  • Preparation of Bills of Quantities
  • Advice on tendering and procurement options
  • Tender documentation, administration, evaluation, and preparation of tender reports
  • Contract Administration

POST- CONTRACT

  • Contractual advice on the rights and obligations of parties
  • Cash flow forecasts
  • Assessment of ongoing works and preparation of interim valuations
  • Cost control and advice on cost implications of variations
  • Preparation of financial appraisals
  • Preparation of final accounts

SPECIALIZED SERVICES

  • • Conducting feasibility studies
  • Dilapidation assessment and reports
  • Technical audit of executed projects
