EVANS WANJALA SIMIYU

MANAGING DIRECTOR

About me

Qs. Evans holds a Master’s Degree in Construction Project Management from the University of Liverpool and a Bachelor’s Degree in Quantity Surveying from the University of Nairobi. He oversees day-to-day company operations as the Managing Director. Qs Evans has over 18 years of immense experience in the construction industry both as a Quantity Surveyor and a Project Manager at the local and international levels. Qs. Evans is a Chartered Quantity Surveyor with the Royal Institute of Charted Surveyors (MRICS) as well as a registered Quantity Surveyor in Kenya. He has over 18 years of post-graduation experience in quantity surveying and project management, nationally and internationally. He has more than 10 years in consultancy (Kenya), 2 years with the main contractor (Seychelles), and 6 years with the Cayman Islands Government as the main QS/PM consultant. He is an international Chartered Quantity Surveyor having registered with the Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors (MRICS) and specializes in overall project management and quantity surveying responsibilities. Qs Evans also brings a wealth of experience in project and facilities management, having obtained a master’s degree in project management and practiced for the last 15 years, which gives him requisite skills in tackling challenges in the construction, property, and facilities management field. This ensures the delivery of projects (institutional, residential, and commercial) within budget, time, and quality. Qs Evans is a confident, self-driven, analytical, and hardworking professional, and a leader with a practical hands-on approach who is always determined to achieve the best results and meet set objectives. He has high esteem for quality results and this therefore always ready to learn new skills. QS Evans utilizes the latest information technology to provide quantity surveying services and manage projects to achieve accurate and reliable results using professional market software and programs such as - Cubicost, Vector, Cubit, Office Projects, Planswift, Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Office, Smartsheet and Bluebeam Revue.

QS CHARLES K. MAINGI

DIRECTOR, PROJECT MANAGEMENT, AND MARKETING DEPARTMENTS

About me

Qs. Charles holds a degree in Building Economics from the University of Nairobi and is registered as a Professional Quantity Surveyor with the Board of Registration of Architects and Quantity Surveyors (BORAQS). Qs. Charles is a seasoned Quantity Surveyor and Project Manager with over 30 years of experience, having managed feasibility study works and performed technical management roles in the execution of small, large, and mega projects. Notably, he has been involved in the following projects; Greenspan residences in Kenya, University of Global Health Equity in Kigali Rwanda Mega mixed-use development in Dare Salaam Tanzania, Brookhouse School Kenya and multiple defense forces projects in Kenya. He has also been involved in various arbitration projects including sitting as a member of the procurement appeals board. QS. Charles is the Director in charge of the Project Management Department working with our Clients to ensure projects are completed on time, within budget, and achieving quality in accordance with the specifications outlined in the drawings and construction details
